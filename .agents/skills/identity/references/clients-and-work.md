## How We Talk About Our Work

Lead with what the client needed, not with us. Show the outcome, not the process (unless process is the point). Use concrete numbers and proof points when available.

When describing projects, follow this structure:
1. What the client does (one line)
2. What they needed
3. What we made
4. The result (if shareable)
