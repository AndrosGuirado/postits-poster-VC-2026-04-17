# Feels Like Studio — Identity

## Who We Are

Feels Like is a Design and Innovation Studio working between Los Angeles and Barcelona. We push the boundaries of what interfaces can be, how they work, and how they make you feel.

Founded as part of the B-Reel family. Independent in spirit, collaborative by nature.

## What We Do

Two modes of working:

**Thinking** — Strategy, research, concept development. We help clients figure out what to build and why.

**Making** — Design, engineering, motion, graphics. We build the thing. End-to-end or modular, depending on what the project needs.

We work across brand platforms, product interfaces, campaigns, and experimental projects. Most of our work lives at the intersection of design and technology.

## Brand Personality

Friendly, inventive, a little Swedish.

- Confident, not arrogant
- Friendly and approachable, not stiff
- Humble and passionate, not self-important
- Inventive and nerdy, not gimmicky
- Direct, not blunt
- Warm, not sentimental

## Core Beliefs

**Emotion** — Build experiences that elicit emotional response. Every detail, every micro-interaction matters.

**Intelligence** — Human and artificial intelligence working together. Technology in service of craft.

**Art** — Balance data and intuition to make things magic. The best work comes from both.

## Culture

- **Flexible** — End-to-end, 0-to-1, or modular support. We adapt to what the project needs.
- **Not afraid of the dark** — Comfortable with unknown territory. We figure things out.
- **Design ++ Engineering** — Cross-disciplinary by default. Designers code, engineers design-think.
