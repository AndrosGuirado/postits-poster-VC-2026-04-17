# Feels Like Studio — Visual Guidelines

## Design Philosophy

Interface is Brand. Every pixel, interaction, and transition communicates who we are. Our visual language is clean, confident, and quietly inventive.

We don't follow trends for the sake of it. We design things that feel right and age well.

## Imagery & Photography

- Authentic over stock. Real people, real situations.
- Clean composition. Space to breathe.
- Avoid clichés: no handshakes, no people pointing at screens, no forced diversity.
- When in doubt, less is more.

## Layout Principles

- Generous white space. Let content breathe.
- Clear visual hierarchy. The eye should know where to go.
- Grid-based but not rigid. Structure with room to play.
- Consistency across touchpoints. A deck should feel like the website should feel like an email.

## Motion & Animation

- Purposeful, not decorative. Movement should communicate something.
- Smooth and natural easing. No harsh or mechanical transitions.
- Subtle by default. Reserved moments of delight.
