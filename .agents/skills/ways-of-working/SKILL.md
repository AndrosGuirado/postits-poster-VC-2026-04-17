# Feels Like Studio — Ways of Working

## How We Structure Work

### Two Modes

**Thinking** — Research, strategy, concept. Usually front-loaded in a project. Involves smaller teams: strategy, design lead, CD.

**Making** — Design, build, ship. Cross-disciplinary teams: design, engineering, production, motion. Scales up and down based on project phase.

Most projects move through both. Some clients come to us for one or the other.

## Collaboration Norms

- **Default to async.** Write it down before scheduling a meeting.
- **Thread in Slack.** Keep channels clean. Use threads for depth.
- **Barcelona and LA overlap.** Be mindful of the time gap. Core overlap hours: <!-- TODO: confirm --> roughly 4pm-7pm CET / 7am-10am PT.
- **Share early, share often.** Work in progress is welcome. Don't polish before showing.
- **Credit the team.** When sharing work externally, acknowledge who contributed.

## Client Engagement

- **We adapt to the client's tools** when it makes sense, but keep our internal workflow consistent.
- **One producer per project** as the client's main point of contact for logistics and timelines.
- **Creative leads present the work.** Producers coordinate, creatives sell.
